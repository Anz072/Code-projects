Wojack Inc.

The image originated in 2009 on a Polish imageboard called vichan, and was later reposted to the German/international imageboard krautchan in 2010 by a Polish poster called "wojak."

It grew to popularity on 4chan, where the character became associated with the phrases formerly used by wojak such as "that feel", "that feel when", and "I know that feel, bro". Countless variants of the meme have since emerged, expressing different emotions or representing various archetypes.
